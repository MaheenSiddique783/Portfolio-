    Hey there — I'm Maheen Siddique, a curious mix of engineer, designer, and serial tinkerer.

    I love crafting experiences that live both in the real world (like automated fish feeders powered by STM32 boards) and the digital one (like clean, responsive websites built with WordPress, Figma, and just enough JavaScript to keep things interesting).

    Currently pursuing a B.Sc. in Software Systems Engineering from University of Regina, I thrive in the space where software logic meets intuitive UI. I believe a good design should not only work — it should make people smile.

    When I’m not wrangling microcontrollers or polishing interfaces, you’ll probably find me sketching UI mockups, reverse-engineering everyday gadgets, or quietly celebrating a perfectly aligned CSS grid.
